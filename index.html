Why Us?

Does this sound familiar?

  • You are sitting on surveys, donor lists, or reviews you never actually use.
  • You are unsure why your audience or customers are disengaging.
  • You are struggling to translate messy spreadsheets into a plain-English strategy.

    • Most organizations don't lack data; they lack translation. You don't need a massive consulting firm, and you don't need another expensive, confusing software dashboard. You just need someone to deep-clean the information you already have and tell you exactly what it means.

    Doctoral-Level Rigor. Plain-English Results

    That is where Waters Analytics comes in. We don't just skim your spreadsheets; we use advanced statistics and rigorous quantitative analysis to uncover the truth hiding in your numbers.

    Whether we are building predictive models to forecast student retention, running complex behavioral correlations to see what truly drives donor giving, or conducting a multivariable analysis of your customer satisfaction, the methodology applied to your data is strictly academic-grade.

    But here is the difference: you will never have to decipher the math. We run the complex statistical models behind the scenes so that what is delivered to your desk is a clear, actionable strategy. We do the heavy quantitative lifting so you can focus on leadership.

    Services

    Every service we offer is built on rigorous, Ph.D.-level research methods and delivered in a clear, plain-English report. Our goal is to translate your complex or underutilized data into actionable outcomes, providing you with the clarity needed to make smarter business decisions.

    The Audience Insight Audit

    The perfect starting point for organizations with existing, underutilized data.

    We take a messy dataset you have been "meaning to get to" and deep-clean it to ensure complete accuracy, returning a standardized, "Ready-to-Analyze" file. You receive a concise executive summary that identifies hidden retention risks and outlines plain-English next steps.

    • Ideal For: Private schools, local nonprofits, and growing service businesses.
    • Investment: $800 – $995

    The Customer Insight Report

    Move beyond simple spreadsheets into deep behavioral analysis.

    We help design valid survey questions, analyze the collected responses, and identify your high-value customers and their behavioral patterns. We translate the findings into actionable insights, pinpointing exactly what your highest-spending customers love or hate about your business.

    • Ideal For: Local retailers, restaurants, boutique agencies, or niche e-commerce sites.
    • Investment: $750 – $1,500 per project

    The Retention Risk Model

    A predictive analysis to stop the "leaky bucket" of customer or student churn.

    We run a predictive risk analysis—such as a logistic regression or survival analysis—to determine exactly which variables predict attrition. You receive a "Risk Profile" PDF that clearly defines who is leaving and why, empowering your organization to intervene months in advance.

    • Ideal For: Membership models (like health clubs) and private schools.
    • Investment: $1,000 – $3,000

    The "Hidden Gold" Donor Audit

    A specialized service designed to pay for itself by recovering major contributors.

    Using Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value (RFM) modeling, we segment your past donor history to identify "Sleeping Giants" (disengaged high-capacity donors) and "Hidden Loyalists" (small-dollar donors ready for a major ask). The package includes data cleaning, a 5-page "State of the Union" executive summary, a prioritized call list of 25-50 prospects, and a 60-minute strategy debrief.

    • Ideal For: Directors of Development at private schools and large nonprofits.
    • Investment: $2,800 – $3,000 flat fee

    Audience Insight + Messaging Diagnostic

    A premium, comprehensive audit for organizations needing deep strategic alignment.

    A comprehensive audit focusing strictly on messaging effectiveness and trust and reputation measurement systems. We deliver the ultimate outcomes of clarity, trust, and better strategic decisions, providing doctoral-level guidance rather than just raw data analysis.

    • Ideal For: Established organizations looking to overhaul their foundational strategy.
    • Investment: $5,000

    The Fractional Advisory Retainer

    Ongoing, predictive oversight for a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire.

    We act as a part-time member of your leadership team, providing the caliber of insights a full-time executive would. You receive consistent peace of mind through ongoing reporting, strategic oversight, and regular check-ins.

    About Waters Analytics

    Doctoral Precision for Practical Business Decisions.

    I am Dr. Stephenson Waters, and I believe that data should never just be a static report; it should be a powerful tool for clarity. Waters Analytics was founded on the principle that many organizations, while capable of collecting data, lack the analytical framework to truly use it for strategic advantage.

    We do not just find numbers. We architecturalize insight.

    Our Foundation: Rigor + Plain English

    With a background as a journalism educator and researcher, I specialize in the systematic process of gathering information and making it accessible. My Ph.D. research is deeply rooted in media effects and psychology, providing me with unique frameworks to predict human behavioral patterns and measure complex concepts like trust and reputation.

    I bring that same academic rigor and multi-level modeling approach to private institutions, nonprofits, and service businesses, but with a critical difference: No academic jargon. You get the predictive power and deep analysis of doctoral-level methods, translated into clear, plain-English executive summaries and actionable plans.

    Why Choose a Doctor for Your Data?

    • Valid Measurement: We don't just count clicks. We ensure the data we analyze actually measures the core concept of interest—whether that is donor intent, student satisfaction, or retention risk.
    • Predictive Insight: We move beyond descriptive statistics ("What happened?") to predictive and diagnostic analytics ("Why did it happen, and what will happen next?").
    • Trust-Focused Strategy: Based on specialized research, we place a high value on measuring and auditing the core metrics of organization-stakeholder relationships: trust and reputation.

    Your Data. Your Blueprint. Your Outcomes.

    At Waters Analytics, your data is treated as a blueprint. My goal is to synthesize your unique information into a structure of clarity, delivering outcomes that directly drive better strategic decisions. Every project is an act of meticulous insight construction, turning uncertainty into actionable opportunity.

    If you are ready to use Ph.D.-level methodology to build clarity and predictable success, I invite you to contact us for a consultation. Let's make your data build something significant.
